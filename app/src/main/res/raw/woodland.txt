Take a stroll through Woodland Park Crossing, Fairfax County's premier one-stop shopping destination. Day and night, weekdays and weekends, Woodland Park boasts some of the best eateries, fitness centers and retailers in the Herndon and Reston area. With paved sidewalks and classic streetlamps, Woodland Park has created a small-town atmosphere that's pedestrian-friendly and ideal for shoppers of all ages.
